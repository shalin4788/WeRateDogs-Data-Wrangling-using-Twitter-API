Insights¶

On the original dataset

  • There is no tweet with image predictions beyond Aug 1 , 2017
  • The original dataset itself had retweets with image predictions filtered out, so the original dataset didn't require filtering out retweet data explicitly

On the cleaned dataframe

  • 100% tweets had dog prediction as true since the predictions that had p_dog = false as a part of either of the 3 algorithms were filtered out from the final dataframe
  • 49% of tweets were cleaned due to data wrangling exercise - either due to completeness, validity, accuracy or consistency issues
  • For the dog prediction, the maximum confidence level is 0.33 whereas 75% of the cleaned tweets had prediction confidence of 0.326
  • Golden retriever was the dog breed that was most popular among tweets as predicted by the algorithm(s) - Close to 127 tweets, whereas there were a few dog breeds as predicted by alogrithm as least popular among tweets i.e.scotch terrier, irish water spaniel
  • Labrador retriever had the best rating, whereas an entlebucher got the lowest rating
